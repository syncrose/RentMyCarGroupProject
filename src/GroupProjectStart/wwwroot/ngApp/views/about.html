RentMyCar is a collaborative effort among programmers from Seattle, WA and Houston, TX.

The vision for this unique APP is to demonstrate our newly acquired programming skills and to demonstrate our ability to work on a team using Visual Studio Team Services to build a tool displaying some of the latest tools available in the market.
